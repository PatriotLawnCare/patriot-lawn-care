Who We Are

Patriot Lawn Care was founded by veterans with a deep commitment to service — a commitment that didn’t end when the uniform came off. We saw lawn care not just as a job, but as a way to serve our communities again, this time with blades of grass instead of boots on the ground.

Our Values

  • Integrity: We do what we say, every time.
  • Discipline: Our attention to detail reflects our military background.
  • Respect: For your property, your time, and your trust.
  • Community: We live and work in the neighborhoods we serve.

Why We Hire Veterans

Veterans bring unmatched reliability, accountability, and pride in their work. Patriot Lawn Care proudly employs veterans because we know firsthand what they’re capable of — and how they lift up the communities around them.
